We measure wins by solving client challenges effectively, not by how many hours we log. Real impact is our metric.

What to Expect

Our Hiring Promise: Transparent, Rigorous, Human

We believe the hiring process should be as thoughtful as the work we do. Here's what to expect when you apply to join MYND.

1

Discovery Call (30 mins)

We align your goals with our mission—no trick questions, just real conversations. We want to understand your aspirations and how MYND fits into your career journey.

2

Skills Assessment

Tackle a genuine client challenge—understand the client situation through the hiring manager and answer using your skills and experience. This gives us insight into your approach and thought process.

3

Client Interview

Meet the people you'd work for daily. We care about synergy and shared excitement for what's possible. This happens only in some of our many niche client engagements.

4

Offer & Onboarding

You'll get a personalised welcome plan, including a buddy pairing and an overview of your first 90 days. We ensure a smooth transition into your new role at MYND.

No Ghosting, No Gimmicks

We value your time. We'll keep you posted at every step—because a transparent hiring journey is non-negotiable at MYND.
